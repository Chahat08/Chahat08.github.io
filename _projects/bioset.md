BioSET is a visualization tool for biomarker-based spatial co-expression analysis in tumor environments. It was awarded the **Award of Excellence** at the Bio+MedVis Challenge at IEEE VIS 2025.

## Motivation

Understanding how biomarkers are spatially co-expressed in tumor microenvironments is crucial for developing targeted therapies and understanding cancer progression.

## Approach

BioSET provides coordinated views that allow researchers to explore spatial relationships between biomarkers across tissue samples, combining statistical analysis with interactive visualization.

## Results

The tool was presented at IEEE VIS 2025 and received the Award of Excellence at the Bio+MedVis Challenge, recognizing its contribution to biomedical data visualization.
